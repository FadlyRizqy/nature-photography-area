Nature Photography Area

NPA

Nature photography captures the beauty and essence of the natural world. It showcases the intricate details of flora and fauna, the grandeur of landscapes, and the mesmerizing play of light and shadow. Nature Photography Area (NPA) is a platform that can be used to showcase your photography work. The competition is open to everyone, amateurs and professionals alike.

Rules


By entering the competition you hereby accept the Competition Rules:


Eligibility


Everyone can enter, provided that the photographed item is located within Indonesia. The copyright of the photo remains yours, but NPA has the right to publish your photo on the NPA's Website and NPA's Instagram page.


Submission entry starts on April 1, 2023 09:00 WIB and ends on April 16, 2023 09:00 WIB, with each user can only submit one individual images and no registration fee.


To enter the competition, you can upload your photo via Google Form by clicking here.


Images Requirements


  • No Watermarks / Copyright Units / Logos on images;
  • Saved as jpeg/jpg
  • Minimum resolution of 72dpi
  • Landscape dimension
  • Digital file should not exceed 5MB
  • Photos may be taken with digital cameras or cell phones.

    • All entries must be the original work of the entrant and must not infringe the rights of any other party. The entrant must be the sole copyright owner of all photographs entered and must have obtained permission of any people featured in the entries (or their parents/guardians if children under 12 are featured).


    Judging


    Judging starts on April 17, 2023 09:00 WIB and ends on April 23, 2023 23:59 WIB. The submitted photos will be assessed through voting by people, voting will be done through google form and likes on Instagram. Voting points are 65% on google form and voting points through instagram likes are 35%.

    If in the future, cheating is found on the photos in the competition (especially on Instagram likes), NPA has the right to disqualify the participant's photos.


    Competition Prizes


    Winner information will be notified via email on May 1, 2023 and the winner's submission photo and name will appear on the NPA's Website.


    The first winner will receive:
    Rp100.000,00 cash prize


    Second winner will receive:
    Rp50.000,00 cash prize


    Cash prizes will be paid via bank transfer or other electronic methods. Any fees incurred for foreign currency transfers will be deducted from the prize amount.


    Conditions


    NPA reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify and remove any entry that does not comply with the following requirements, even after the entry is submitted on the Website. You warrant and represent, in respect of each entry submitted by You (''Entry''), as follows:
    1. You are the sole owner and author of each Entry.
    2. You have the right to make Your entry available to the Competition.
    3. Each Entry does not contain personally identifiable information about You or any other person(s).
    4. Each Entry does not contain any infringing, threatening, false, misleading, abusive, harassing, libelous, defamatory, vulgar, obscene, scandalous, inflammatory, pornographic or profane content.
    5. Each Entry does not contain any material that could constitute or encourage conduct which would be considered a criminal offense, give rise to civil liability, or otherwise violate any law.

    NPA recognizes you, the author of the image, retain full ownership of the copyright in each Entry. The full name of each entrant whose work is selected as a winner or finalist will be published online after the competition ended.

    You hereby grant NPA a non-exclusive license in each Entry in all media for any use connected to the promotion of the Competition, including, but not limited too:
    (a) judging the Competition.
    (b) displaying the winning entries and runners up at NPA's Website.
    (c) inclusion within the NPA's Website and Instagram.
    (d) allowing viewers of the Website to view images on a computer screen, PDA or mobile telephone and store such pages in electronic form on disk or on a PDA or mobile telephone (but not on any server or other storage device connected to a network) for their personal, non-commercial use only.
